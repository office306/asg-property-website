Our rent-to-rent service is designed for suitable landlords who want a clear agreement, consistent communication and reduced day-to-day involvement. Each property is assessed individually before terms are proposed.

Every property and situation is assessed individually. Any arrangement is subject to suitability, due diligence and a written agreement.

What the arrangement may include

  • An agreed rental term
  • Clear responsibility for day-to-day management
  • Regular inspections and condition monitoring
  • Agreed small maintenance responsibilities

Why landlords consider rent to rent

  • Potentially more predictable income
  • Reduced exposure to changeover voids
  • Less time spent managing occupancy

Our approach

  • We explain the arrangement clearly
  • We assess suitability before making promises
  • We document responsibilities in writing
