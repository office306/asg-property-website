Guaranteed rent for landlords who value certainty.

ASG Property offers guaranteed-rent arrangements for suitable properties across Cambridgeshire and surrounding areas. Subject to assessment and agreement, landlords may receive consistent rent for up to five years, with no void periods during the agreed term and support for agreed small maintenance items.

What guaranteed rent can provide

  • Predictable monthly rent for the agreed term
  • No rental voids during the agreed arrangement
  • A single professional point of contact
  • Regular property inspections and oversight
  • Agreed small maintenance support

How the process works

  • Initial conversation
  • Property suitability review
  • Clear written proposal
  • Agreement and preparation
  • Ongoing oversight

Who it may suit

  • Landlords wanting predictable income
  • Owners moving abroad
  • Landlords reducing management responsibilities
  • Owners with suitable properties in our areas
