Frequently asked questions

Useful answers before we speak.

What types of property opportunities do you consider?

We consider a range of residential and value-add opportunities, including buy-to-let, refurbishment, BRR, HMO and resale strategies. Suitability depends on the location, numbers, condition and intended exit.

How does the guaranteed-rent service work?

For suitable properties, ASG Property becomes the contractual tenant under an agreed rent-to-rent arrangement. We pay the agreed monthly rent, manage occupiers and handle the agreed day-to-day responsibilities for the contract term.

Is five years guaranteed for every property?

We can offer agreements of up to five years. The final term, rent and responsibilities depend on the property, inspection, compliance checks and the signed agreement.

What maintenance does ASG cover?

We can cover minor day-to-day maintenance as set out in the agreement. Major, structural and statutory landlord responsibilities are agreed separately and remain subject to the contract and applicable law.

Do you buy properties directly?

Depending on the property and circumstances, a direct purchase or another flexible sale route may be considered. The first step is a confidential conversation about the property and your preferred timescale.

Which areas do you cover?

Our core focus is the East of England, including Cambridgeshire, Bedfordshire, Northamptonshire and selected surrounding locations.

How do I register my investment criteria?

Complete the enquiry form with your target area, budget, strategy and timescale. ASG Property Ltd can then assess whether suitable opportunities align with your objectives.

Is an initial conversation confidential?

Yes. Enquiries are handled directly and discreetly, with no pressure to proceed.
